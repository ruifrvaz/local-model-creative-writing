### Dialogue-Heavy Scene Guidelines

**Challenge:** Social gatherings = extended conversations  
**Solution:** Preserve complete exchanges, even if slightly longer

**DO:**
✅ Keep multi-person conversations intact (A asks → B responds → C interjects)  
✅ Preserve back-and-forth dynamics (arguments, flirtations, jokes)  
✅ Include enough context for standalone reading (who's speaking, what prompted topic)  
✅ End chunks on natural pauses (topic exhaustion, character exits, interruptions)  

**DON'T:**
❌ Split mid-exchange (A asks question → chunk ends before B answers)  
❌ Orphan responses (B's response without A's question context)  
❌ Break emotional beats (setup → punchline, tension → release)  
❌ Separate cause-effect (character says something hurtful → chunk ends before reaction)

**Acceptable Chunk Size for Dialogue:**
- If complete conversation requires 2,200-2,400 words: **KEEP INTACT**
- Better one 2,300-word chunk than two 1,150-word chunks with broken exchanges
