## Pattern Formulas

### Memory Opening Pattern
```
[Ordinal] memory - [Thematic Title]

''[Philosophical quote or historical excerpt]''

[Source attribution];

[Scene establishment]
```

### Scene Break Pattern
```
[Forward momentum or emotional beat]

***

[New POV/location/time establishment]
```

### Dialogue Pattern
```
'[Speech],' [character] [tag] [optional: physical detail].
[Beat or reaction]
'[Response],' [second character] [tag].
```

### Internal Monologue Pattern
```
[Narrative context]
...[first-person cosmic/philosophical observation]...
[Return to second-person]
```

### Emotional Show-Don't-Tell Pattern
```
AVOID: "He felt scared"
USE: "Blood drained from your cheeks and your eyes widened in panic"
```

## Character Voice Markers

### Alan (Protagonist "You")
- Informal, questioning authority
- "I am not afraid of falling! I just don't like stepping into...nothingness."
- Impulsive actions, emotional reactions

### Haji (Logical Friend)
- Formal greetings ("Salutations")
- Statistical reasoning ("probability of falling is close to zero")
- Dry humor with deadpan delivery

### Authority Figures (Mentors, Specialists)
- Bureaucratic language
- References to Directives
- Credit penalties, formal conduct

### Von Howitt (Mentor Figure)
- Parental, sacrificial tone
- "My dear, since your birth I took care of you as a father would a daughter"
- Grave emotional undertones

## Worldbuilding Integration Rules

### DO: Natural Immersion
```
"you hovered your Units over its touchscreens"
"the eternium coils of your hoverboard"
```

### DON'T: Explicit Definitions
```
AVOID: "Units are small devices that pyreans use to..."
```

### Technical Term Density
- ~2-3 terms per 100 words
- Higher in exposition scenes
- Lower in emotional/action scenes
